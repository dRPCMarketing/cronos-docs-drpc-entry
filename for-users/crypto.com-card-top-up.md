# 💳 Crypto.com Card Top Up

### **Simplify Your Crypto.com Card Top-Up with Cronos EVM Chain**

We’re thrilled to introduce a feature that makes funding your Crypto.com card easier than ever. By linking your non-custodial wallet directly to your card, you can top up quickly and conveniently. Follow these simple steps to get started!



### How to Link Your Wallet

1\. Visit[ ](https://zkevm.cronos.org/)[https://cronos.org/card](https://cronos.org/card).&#x20;

### **How to Top Up Your Crypto.com Card**

1\. Select the token you wish to use and enter the amount you’d like to top up. If you choose a token other than USDC or ETH, it will automatically be swapped to the best available rate for USDC or ETH via [VVS Finance](https://vvs.finance/swap). Please note that additional fees and slippage may apply. Confirm the transaction by signing the approval request in your wallet.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfGAFlmR2pYopSthWF40FX7Bl-y-iFTGJGfxvgyT2PK2ezGi9ontAa2UIE_FU0pDj6TlyYsJWaTNRwZdpfXG7WjLdkpF8NQOQw4WjOPHrXQwBQ8n4F0maxFXCvrc8ny9vs_jjkC6g?key=no55sTNt3DKXQ5Zgf-0lIA)

2\. Once the transaction is processed, you’ll see a "Transaction Successful" notification. It may take a few minutes to send the fund to the Crypto.com Card.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdptfCCVkjD1ksUd-x6FX8Hj3JohPMT108AUTWfORE70M04k4jPr4pPNJpiuhieH3wiJOUQ94aeLsKA1gUMeIzfrBpNUvHSblsqpcnwbWZDlqEZN_FJKMio_WUUvD6yAkLvREvi?key=no55sTNt3DKXQ5Zgf-0lIA)

3\. You can view your top-up history in the History tab of the platform.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdCfrJ_N3XqpaoGNk3z4Lj0YpboyV0jAb-WGk93zDpKI25usOZ6ww4PiiBY7zTJF9h1huPaYOFhI275D7A_FReOgjZ7Rx8_c9wLmlap--KH6F5L8uU5BMC6j9vgIZ7MSat6gd7D?key=no55sTNt3DKXQ5Zgf-0lIA)

### How to Unlink Your Wallet

1\. Go to the wallet linking page on the platform.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXefxZnOCOO3RS48DqrFpV21-1RmoScle5L2rmmN2H2_xvijdToEJK_AlWLPf0lQ80ZdrvixDzzATVX12_zdmtBGcVYcTunxOIyQQoaG-d8zABrXP7X66UrEVVL3jgDEoPywDIFd9w?key=no55sTNt3DKXQ5Zgf-0lIA)

2\. Click "Unlink", and authorise the disconnection of your non-custodial wallet to your Crypto.com deposit address.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeI7ueK5W_QuIERx79QU9keSOxU-g-qAYtXLB70lKBiaMZJ0D7BRVYQxStan3LtxNn10HqL9Ohpodf4VdsL1OV9lsYOf0RZ-mqKcDcKbEUx_C1lSUYEu-MmIZH9dEromLPl7dJUkQ?key=no55sTNt3DKXQ5Zgf-0lIA)

3\. Open the Crypto.com App, navigate to the Card Module, and disable the Top-Up with Cronos EVM/zkEVM Chain option in Card Settings.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfD5chF-eIMivQg7QN6B7prkcUItWTlRN7E9ISHZtag9xMIgT_b-a430Uj-6nBOCkhdic96gcc_5xE_UzwBbBLYreiVEadkGn6DQMvqAZ3HUVF9_qLwSE8OKOvRW-yeP4HT9foAdA?key=no55sTNt3DKXQ5Zgf-0lIA)
