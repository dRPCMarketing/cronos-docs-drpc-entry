# 🏆 Ambassador Ranks

Your Rank as an Ambassador comes with **status badges and recognition** on all our official channels. The higher you move up in ranks, the more responsibility you can take on and the bigger your impact - and privileges - become. Rank reviews by the core team happen periodically and reflect your consistent contribution and impact.



* **🌱 Rookie** — Toolkit access, starter mission, light mentorship.\

* **🔥 Envoy** — Priority missions, occasional swag, feature highlights.\

* **🛡️ Sentinel** — Early previews, mentoring of Rookies and Envoys.\

* **🏆 Elite** — Regional/track lead; co-host rights for events & AMAs.\

* **🧭 Council** — Public program steward; input on proposals/roadmap, selective travel support.
