# 🎓 About the Ambassador Program

### What is the Cronos Ambassador Program?

A structured, scalable initiative that shifts growth from centralized marketing to community-led activation. Ambassadors lead local education, adoption campaigns, and builder support.

PS: thinking about becoming a Cronos ambassador?

👉 Get going by filling out the [**application form**](https://cronos.typeform.com/ambassadors)\


**Who it’s for**

* Creators & educators who explain Cronos clearly and consistently
* Community operators who run welcoming, active spaces
* Developer champions who host technical sessions and mentor teams\


**What you’ll do**

* Propose and ship campaigns (content, events, quests) tailored to your region/audience
* Run beginner-friendly onboarding sessions
* Support builders with meetups, quickstarts, and referrals to programs/grants\


**How we support you**

* Brand kit, templates, event playbooks, referral guidance
* Microgrants for approved campaigns and meetups
* Badges, spotlights, and a clear path to level up your Ambassadorship game
* Direct contact channels for additional support\


### Our objectives

* 🌍 **Grow the Cronos Community #CROfam** — Increase and expand regional presence and host \
  recurring meetups in high-growth markets\

* 🧠 **Educate Users** — Provide clear guides, workshops, and localized resources\

* 🛠️ **Attract Builders** — Mentor and activate credible teams and projects building on Cronos\

* 🗳️ **Decentralize Resources & Impact** — Empower self-initiated, grassroots campaigns\

* 🎯 **Measure ROI** — Track outcomes and measure our impact on the ecosystem\


### What we value and how we operate

1. **Multitrack Specialization** — there are multiple tracks for Ambassadors to choose from, such as content, localization, education, campaigns, champion builders, community ops and more! _(more on that in section Contribution Tracks)_\

2. **Localized Growth Engines** — we want to expand regionally, with localized chapters, translated content and culturally adapted programs\

3. **Ambassador-Led Campaigns** — we want to move fast and nimble: think bottom-up proposals with fast reviews and (potentially) microgrants\

4. **Tiered Incentives** — Ambassadors’ efforts should be rewarded. We do this with a clear progress path based on ranks. Level up from Rookie → Envoy → Sentinel → Elite → Council _(more on that in section Ambassador Ranks)_\
