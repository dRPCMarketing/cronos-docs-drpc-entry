# Our Objectives & Values

### Our objectives

* 🌍 **Grow the Cronos Community #CROfam** — Increase and expand regional presence and host \
  recurring meetups in high-growth markets<br>
* 🧠 **Educate Users** — Provide clear guides, workshops, and localized resources<br>
* 🛠️ **Attract Builders** — Mentor and activate credible teams and projects building on Cronos<br>
* 🗳️ **Decentralize Resources & Impact** — Empower self-initiated, grassroots campaigns<br>
* 🎯 **Measure ROI** — Track outcomes and measure our impact on the ecosystem

### What we value and how we operate

1. **Multitrack Specialization** — there are multiple tracks for Ambassadors to choose from, such as content, localization, education, campaigns, champion builders, community ops and more! _(more on that in section Contribution Tracks)_<br>
2. **Localized Growth Engines** — we want to expand regionally, with localized chapters, translated content and culturally adapted programs<br>
3. **Ambassador-Led Campaigns** — we want to move fast and nimble: think bottom-up proposals with fast reviews and (potentially) microgrants<br>
4. **Tiered Incentives** — Ambassadors’ efforts should be rewarded. We do this with a clear progress path based on ranks. Level up from Rookie → Envoy → Sentinel → Elite → Council _(more on that in section Ambassador Ranks)_
