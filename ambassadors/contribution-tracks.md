# 🏎️ Contribution Tracks

Choose one or multiple tracks as a Cronos Ambassador and positively impact the ecosystem by playing to your strengths. We’ve given some examples as to what each track might look like on a day-to-day basis.\


* **Content & Influence** — Threads, explainers, short videos that are accurate, visual, and helpful.\

* **Localization** — Translate/adapt official news and how-tos for your language/culture.\

* **Education & Onboarding** — Beginner workshops and simple, step-by-step flows.\

* **Campaign Architect** — Quests, contests, and social sprints with clear KPIs.\

* **Builder Champion** — Dev meetups, starter repos, and credible builder referrals.\

* **Community Builder** — Meetups, moderation, feedback loops, and welcoming spaces.[\
