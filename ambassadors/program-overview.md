# 🚀 Program Overview

By consistently **completing Missions**, engaging with peers, and embodying the values of the CROfam, ambassadors **earn Points** and can **climb the Ranks season by season**\
— from 🌱 Rookie to 🔥 Envoy, 🛡 Sentinel, 🏆 Elite and eventually 🧭 Council.&#x20;

Ambassadors contribute through **six Tracks**, each tied to Cronos’ growth goals. Every mission is assigned points based on effort and impact, making contributions clear, fair, and easy to track.

The Cronos Ambassador Program is designed as a seasonal journey of growth and leadership. Each season runs for 3 months, at the end of which ambassadors may be promoted to the next rank. The higher the Rank, the **more benefits an Ambassador unlocks**...
