# 🧑‍🚀 Mission Control

### Missions: how to earn points

Missions are certain predefined tasks you can complete to help amplify the reach and success of Cronos.\


* **Get started with Missions** by going to our [**Mission Control**](https://zealy.io/cw/cronosambassadorprogram/invite/rTuvVA45MMribW901_5Ku)\

* Missions are grouped per contribution track (see section Contribution Tracks) such as localization, education, content creation and more.\

* By completing Missions and providing ‘Proof of Work’ \*wink nudge\*, you become eligible to earn points…\

* …**each Mission earns you a certain amount of points**, which all go towards your total points needed to go up in Rank. _(The core team holds the right to attribute additional points on top of the Mission points to certain Ambassadors who have proven to be extremely impactful.)_\

* Check out the Ambassador Leaderboard where you can check your points in the overall Ambassador rankings.

### Moving up in Rank

Move up in rank by completing Missions specified in Mission Control. By accruing points and meeting the required amount of points, you can **move up in Rank** at the end of each review period.

**Points required per rank**

* 🌱 Rookie — 50
* 🔥 Envoy — 100
* 🛡️ Sentinel — 150
* 🏆 Elite — 250
* 🧭 Council – 500\
  \
  &#xNAN;_&#x46;YI - you cannot move up more than one rank per review period. E.g. you can’t go from Rookie to Elite, even if you hit the required points threshold for Elite in that one period._
