# DWELLIR

## Introduction

DWELLIR provides reliable, scalable RPC infrastructure that connects your dApp or Web3 project to any blockchain network. With support for over 150 blockchains including Cronos, DWELLIR delivers high-speed, cost-effective infrastructure designed specifically for Web3 developers.

The platform eliminates the complexity of multi-chain integration by offering a single solution that spans 150+ blockchains with regular new network additions. DWELLIR's enterprise-grade infrastructure handles millions of daily requests while maintaining 99.99% uptime through redundant systems across multiple global regions.

With strategically distributed data centers ensuring sub-100ms latency and comprehensive monitoring tools, DWELLIR provides the performance and reliability needed for mission-critical Web3 applications. Whether you're managing traffic spikes or expanding to new chains, DWELLIR's scalable infrastructure grows with your project's needs.

## How does DWELLIR do it?

Reduced latency, lower costs, and more chains like Cronos — DWELLIR's scalable RPC nodes power Web3 developers with high-speed, affordable infrastructure.&#x20;
